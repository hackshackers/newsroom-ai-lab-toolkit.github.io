# Talk to real humans

In this stage we focus on collecting a broad mix of real life experiences ***without worrying about what a solution would look like or whether a solutions is even possible.***

By the end of this stage, you’ll have a set of real-life, specific experiences from people who do or rely on the work of your newsroom, captured into three categories: 

<p>**😣Pain points**: frictions felt or seen</p>
<p>**🐢Inefficiencies**: time sinks, brittle processes, or repetitive steps</p>
<p>**🧙Aspirational opportunities**: things your team and/or readers wish existed, or ideas you’d be excited to explore if there were no constraints.</p> 

<p>Collecting a super broad mix of experiences helps everyone better understand your organization by exposing challenges and ideas. Often things come up in this step that someone else on your team has alredy solved - a quick win just from saying things aloud!</p>
