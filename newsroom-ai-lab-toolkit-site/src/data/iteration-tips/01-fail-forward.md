---
title: "\"Fail\" forward ▶️"
body: |
  <ul>
    <li>Purposefully and deliberately <strong>use "failure" to find success</strong> - perfect is the enemy of the good enough</li>
    <li>Over time failing forward helps us get better at mitigating risk
      <ul>
        <li>… BUT there will always need to be some <strong>tolerance for risk</strong></li>
        <li><strong>Re-evaluate after each iteration</strong> (eg 2-week sprint)
          <ul>
            <li>This keeps risks from growing like they might if the only evaluation happened at the end of the project</li>
          </ul>
        </li>
      </ul>
    </li>
  </ul>
---
