---
title: "⭐Keep focused on the north star, but don't think too far down the road"
body: |
  <ul>
    <li>A key goal of iterative planning is to <strong>avoid over planning</strong> - since this is often based on 🔮"mythical certainty"</li>
    <li>Each time you come to a <strong>decision point</strong>, consider:
      <ul>
        <li>'what is the best option given what we know at this point, which will minimally limit future options'</li>
        <li>This framing can help ensure iterations are thoughtful and well-engineered</li>
        <li>iterative != scrappy</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>Always <strong>consider what decisions must be made now, vs those that can wait</strong></li>
    <li>Eg:
      <ul>
        <li>Choosing a newsletter's title text design doesn't need to block selecting the stories that will be included</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>BUT, always <strong>consider how each choice made might impact possibilities farther down the line</strong>
      <ul>
        <li>AKA - don't build yourself into a corner by making hasty decisions</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
  </ul>
---
