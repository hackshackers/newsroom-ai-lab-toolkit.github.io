---
title: "☑️Debate, decide, commit (+ document)"
body: |
  <ul>
    <li>Be thoughtful, but don't dwell on having perfect data before making a decision</li>
    <li>Capture why each decision is made and what information you used to come to the conclusion
      <ul>
        <li>This is in service of your future selves and teammates</li>
        <li>Eg: <em>We looked at both X and Y as database options - here are the pro/cons we found for each… We went with Y for A, B, C reasons.</em></li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>Worst case, you commit to the decision for two weeks of work, then get more information which allows you to pivot to a better, more informed option</li>
  </ul>
---
