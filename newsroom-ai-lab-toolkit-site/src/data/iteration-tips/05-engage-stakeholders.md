---
title: "💞Engage stakeholders"
body: |
  <ul>
    <li><strong>The best stakeholders are the ones that are the most continuously invested</strong>
      <ul>
        <li>Feedback on each iteration is a must in order to generate the most valuable final solution</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>Consider scheduling a bi-weekly meeting that aligns with sprint cadence, where you give a demo of changes and progress made, receive feedback, and ask/answer questions</li>
    <li>Consider ways to structure cross-team meetings to ensure you are <strong>showing progress <em>and</em> getting meaningful feedback from users</strong>.</li>
  </ul>
---
