---
section: framework
heading: The Repeatable Process
steps:
  - number: "1/"
    title: Gather Observations
    description: "Talk to people closest to the work - reporters, editors, producers, community members. What challenges, gaps, or shifts keep coming up? Collect the clues and hunches."
  - number: "2/"
    title: Define the Problem
    description: "Turn observations into structured problem statements. Who is affected, what do they need, and why does it matter? Writing it out reveals where your understanding is still fuzzy."
  - number: "3/"
    title: Build a Brief
    description: "Expand the most promising problem statement into a brief - context, impact, success criteria, what's still unknown. This becomes the anchor for every decision that follows."
  - number: "4/"
    title: Prototype & Iterate
    description: "Break the brief into solvable parts. Build quickly, test with real people, learn what works. Repeat until the output is good enough - then move to the next part."
---
