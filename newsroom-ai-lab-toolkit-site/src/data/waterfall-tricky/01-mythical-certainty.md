---
title: "🔮 You are relying on \"mythical certainty\""
body: |
  <ul>
    <li>Assumes you can write a <strong>fully comprehensive spec</strong> before any real work begins</li>
    <li>Writing that spec forces you to guess at things that are <strong>truthfully unknowable</strong> this early on</li>
    <li><strong>High precision, low accuracy</strong> — the plan looks exact, but it's often confidently wrong</li>
    <li>Only works if stakeholders already have a <strong>crystal-clear, fully-articulated</strong> idea of what they need</li>
  </ul>
